I’m excited to share my latest analytics project where I analyzed the business impact of 5G implementation for one of India’s leading telecom providers, Wavecon Telecom 📊
(Part of the #codebasicsvirtualinternship)

📌 Project Overview
Analyzed 9 months of data (Jan–Sep 2022) across 15 major Indian cities to assess the impact of the 5G rollout launched in May 2022.

🔍 Key Insights

📉 Revenue Impact

Overall revenue saw a marginal decline of 0.5% (₹80M) post-5G launch

Pre-5G Revenue: ₹15.98 bn

Post-5G Revenue: ₹15.90 bn

⚠️ User Metrics

Active users declined by 8.3% (loss of ~7M users)

Unsubscriptions increased by 23.5% (1.4M users)

Despite churn, remaining users contributed higher value, indicating a revenue-user disconnect

💡 Strategic Recommendations
✅ Expand high-performing 5G plans
✅ Strengthen retention strategies to reduce churn
✅ Address network quality gaps in underperforming metro cities
✅ Introduce segmented pricing to cater to diverse customer segments
